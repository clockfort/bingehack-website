Hello!

Welcome to BingeHack, a multiplayer-friendly, addictive, competitive variant of NetHack, created by Computer Science House members as part of a continuing tradition of maintaining an excellent NetHack fork.

Notable Features

Notable features over vanilla NetHack 3.4.3 and other forks include:
  • In-Game Achievements
  • Live External Statistics
  • Multiplayer Additions
  • Additional Geeky In-Jokes
  • NCurses Interface
  • More modern codebase

... and much more!

About Us

BingeHack is the natural continuation of a long line of CSH NetHack forks; this current iteration exists mostly through the efforts of the main developers Russell Harmon and Chris Lockfort, with special thanks to occasional but noteworthy contributors Alex Grant and Will Orr. We welcome bug reports, forks, pull requests, patches to known bugs, etc at our GitHub Repository; fear not, we are very active on GitHub and will respond almost immediately.
  • Love what we've done? We do to! We don't want your money, but if you insist, you could donate to the Scholarship Fund we're making for our poorer college student members.
